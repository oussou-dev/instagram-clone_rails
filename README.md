### OTAGRAM (Instagram Replica)

~~~
An online mobile photo-sharing, video-sharing, and social networking service
~~~


##### Try OTAGRAM

* Online  :  [hiptime-app](https://otagram.herokuapp.com)    

* Locally :
    - Clone this repositoy on your desktop
    - In your Terminal :   
                        . bundle install  
                        . rake db:migrate  
                        . rails s  
                        . localhost:3000
